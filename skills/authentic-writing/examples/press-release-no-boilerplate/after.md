# Acme Launches CloudSync

SAN FRANCISCO, May 6, 2026 — Acme today released CloudSync, a tool that keeps files in sync across every device a customer owns, available now to all plans at no extra cost.

CloudSync resolves conflicts automatically and works offline, syncing the moment a connection returns.

"Our customers were stitching together three tools to do this; now it is one tap," said Jordan Lee, Head of Product at Acme.

## About Acme

Acme builds productivity tools used by more than two million people worldwide.
