# Recipe Sharing MVP

## Problem

Home cooks have no quick way to share a recipe with friends and see if they actually cooked it.

## Hypothesis

If we let a cook post one recipe and get a "I made this" tap back, enough will return weekly to prove demand.

## Riskiest assumption

That people will come back to mark "I made this" — not just post once and leave.

## In scope

- Post a recipe (title, steps, one photo).
- An "I made this" tap.

## Out of scope

- Accounts beyond email, comments, ratings, meal planner.

## Success metric

≥30% of posters return within 7 days to mark or post again.
